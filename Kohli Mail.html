Frequently asked questions
1. What is business email?
Business email is built exclusively for teams and organizations that run business communications. Business email is set up using the user's custom domain and adds credibility and professionalism to the brand. Features like group aliases make team collaboration more efficient.
2. How is business email different from personal email?
Personal email works for one-to-one exchanges with friends and family, while business email is best for managing business communications and multiple mailboxes. Business email generally comes with group aliases, higher storage, dedicated migration support, and fine levels of management that help administrators control how email is used across the organization.
3. How can I migrate from my existing provider?
Kohli Mail offers intuitive migration tools, along with 24/7 dedicated migration support to ensure smooth, lossless migration from your existing email provider. Mail also supports Calendar and Contacts sync and migration of PST files.
